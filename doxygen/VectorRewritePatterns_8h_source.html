19 /// [VectorInsertStridedSliceOpDifferentRankRewritePattern]
20 /// =======================================================
21 /// RewritePattern for InsertStridedSliceOp where source and destination vectors
22 /// have different ranks.
23 ///
24 /// When ranks are different, InsertStridedSlice needs to extract a properly
25 /// ranked vector from the destination vector into which to insert. This pattern
26 /// only takes care of this extraction part and forwards the rest to
27 /// [VectorInsertStridedSliceOpSameRankRewritePattern].
28 ///
29 /// For a k-D source and n-D destination vector (k < n), we emit:
30 /// 1. ExtractOp to extract the (unique) (n-1)-D subvector into which to
31 /// insert the k-D source.
32 /// 2. k-D -> (n-1)-D InsertStridedSlice op
33 /// 3. InsertOp that is the reverse of 1.
34 ///
35 /// [VectorInsertStridedSliceOpSameRankRewritePattern]
36 /// ==================================================
37 /// RewritePattern for InsertStridedSliceOp where source and destination vectors
38 /// have the same rank. For each outermost index in the slice:
39 /// begin end stride
40 /// [offset : offset+size*stride : stride]
41 /// 1. ExtractOp one (k-1)-D source subvector and one (n-1)-D dest subvector.
42 /// 2. InsertStridedSlice (k-1)-D into (n-1)-D
43 /// 3. the destination subvector is inserted back in the proper place
44 /// 3. InsertOp that is the reverse of 1.
45 ///
46 /// [VectorExtractStridedSliceOpRewritePattern]
47 /// ===========================================
48 /// Progressive lowering of ExtractStridedSliceOp to either:
49 /// 1. single offset extract as a direct vector::ShuffleOp.
50 /// 2. ExtractOp/ExtractElementOp + lower rank ExtractStridedSliceOp +
51 /// InsertOp/InsertElementOp for the n-D case.
52 void populateVectorInsertExtractStridedSliceTransforms(
53  RewritePatternSet &patterns);
